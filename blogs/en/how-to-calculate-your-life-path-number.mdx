## What a life path number means

Your life path number is a numerology value calculated from your date of birth. It is often used as a high-level theme for temperament, lessons and the kind of growth that repeats through your life.

You can calculate it manually, or use the [Life Path Calculator](/calculator/life-path) for an instant result.

## The basic calculation

Write your birthday as digits, then add every digit together. Keep reducing the sum until you reach a single digit, unless your system preserves master numbers such as 11, 22, 33 or 44.

Example:

August 24, 1993 becomes `8 + 2 + 4 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 3 = 36`.

Then reduce `36` to `3 + 6 = 9`.

The life path number is 9.
